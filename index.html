About our company

Moving company from Montreale, Déménagement Simplex offers a complete set of moving solutions and services to our clients. Whether it is a residential moving, commercial moving, industrial or any other types, we’ve got the experience, the right tools and the right people to carry on the job properly. We have acquired a solid reputation with our clients and this reputation is tested every day.
Our skilled and experienced movers are the face of our company and our most valuable asset. Their professional work is what makes every moving nice and easy for our clients.

At Démenagement Simplex we have the following values :
  • Reliability – as soon as we agree upon a deal with the client, we’ll make our best and put all necessary efforts in order to honour our word.
  • Professionalism –While moving an apartment or an entire factory, we work equally hard to fulfill the client’s needs by adapting ourselves to each and every situation.
  • Punctuality – time is money and it is certain that our clients aren’t losing any of it with us. Each moving is planned in advance and we make sure to avoid any unnecessary delay.
  • Honesty – our client’s goods are manipulated with the outmost care and attention in order to preserve them. From the start to the end of the process, we make a honest job that complies to the highest standards.
There are new trends in the moving area: the client requires an excellent service at an affordable price and that is exactly what we have to offer. Moreover, at Déménagement Simplex we are flexible and accommodate the schedules and the needs of our clients.
